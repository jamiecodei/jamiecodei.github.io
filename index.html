Feminist Susan B. Anthony

Who is Susan B. Anthony? Susan B. Anthony is a feminist who fought for women to have equal pay and the right to vote. During her time women did not have the right to vote while men did. When she thought that it was unfair for women to not vote, she traveled around the world, delivering speeches in favor of women's suffrage and wrote a newspaper with Elizabeth Cady Stanton. After all of Susan B. Anthony's hard work, women finally got the right to vote, but she died 14 years before it was official. Feminist Susan B. Anthony is such an amazing women.
